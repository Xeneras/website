In Navajo culture, a skin-walker(yee naaldlooshii) is a type of harmful witch who has the ability to turn into, possess, or disguise themselves as an animal.The legend of the skinwalkers is not well understood outside of Navajo culture, mostly due to reluctance to discuss the subject with outsiders.

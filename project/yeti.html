Definitions

In the folklore of Nepal, the Yeti is an ape-like entity, taller than the an average human, that is said to inhabit the Himalayan region of Nepal, Bhutan and Tibet. The names yeti and Meh-Teh are commonly used by the people indigenous to the region, and are part of their history and mythology. Stories of the Yeti first emerged as a facet of Western popular culture in the 19th century.

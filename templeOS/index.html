TempleOS

Background:

TempleOS was made by Terry A. Davis. Terry A. Davis (1969-2018) was intially diagnosed with bipolar disorder, but he was later declared schizophrenic. Due to this mental disorder, he was unemployed for the rest of his life.


Some basic commands :-

  • If you press shift F1, then it shows you a little about the author.
  • To view directories and file you type in Dir;
  • Pressing shift f6 will give u an empty document where if you press space it draws random lines.
  • Shift f7 will show you some bible lines

What kind of hardware does your OS require?
===TempleOS is a 64-bit, non-preemptive multi-tasking, mutli-cored, public domain, open source, ring-O-only, single address space, non-networked, PC operating system for recreational programming. It supports ISO 9660, FAT32 and RedSea file systems.

How can you get it running?
===It needs KVM to boot it / You need to have a KVM(Kernel-based virtual machine) installed in your computer.
